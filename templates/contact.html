Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most events. For weddings and large corporate events, we suggest 2-3 months advance booking to ensure availability and proper planning.

Do you provide venues or just event management?

We provide comprehensive event management services including venue sourcing, decoration, catering coordination, and full event execution. We work with a network of trusted venues and can help find the perfect location for your event.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refund for cancellations made 48+ hours before the event date. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. Day-of cancellations are non-refundable unless due to emergencies.

Can you customize packages according to my needs?

Absolutely! All our packages can be customized to fit your specific requirements, budget, and preferences. Contact us for a personalized consultation and custom quote.
